Slide 02 · Problem Statement

The Crisis We Face

Urban waste management is failing cities worldwide

🗑️

Overflowing Garbage

Bins overflow before collection, leading to unhygienic and hazardous conditions in urban areas.

⏳

Delayed Resolution

Complaint resolution takes days or weeks with no transparency, frustrating citizens.

🔗

Poor Coordination

Lack of real-time communication between citizens and municipal authorities creates critical gaps.

☣️

Health & Environmental Risks

Uncollected waste spreads disease, pollutes water, and degrades environmental quality.
